Hands-On Reading

Welcome to Hands-On Reading. Made as part of an AHRC project at Newcastle University

After closing this window, please draw on the digital canvas. This works best with a tablet and pen if you have one.

When you feel ready, click the feedback button to answer a few questions on your experience. This app examines how we handwrite and how we read, so please note that your edited canvas will be automatically sent to us when you hit "feedback".

Clicking 'Reset' does not save or send us the edited canvas.
There is also no way for you to pause and resume.

If you do not wish to share your edited canvas or feedback with us, simply click reset.
